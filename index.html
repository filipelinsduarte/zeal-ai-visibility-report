Prompt Deep-Dive

The One Prompt Where Zeal Wins

👤
What software adds CRM features to payment terminals?
ChatGPT

There isn't just one piece of software that "adds CRM to payment terminals" — it's usually done through POS systems with built-in CRM or CRM integrations layered onto payment terminals.

🧩 1. POS systems with built-in CRM

  • Lightspeed POS — tracks customer purchase history, segments customers, runs promotions
  • Clover POS — integrates payments + customer profiles + loyalty tools
  • TouchBistro — CRM + loyalty + tableside payment
  • Shopify POS — syncs in-store payments with customer profiles

🔗 2. CRM-integrated payment software

Connect existing terminals to CRM via integrations. Payment data flows into CRM automatically.

📱 3. Terminal-level CRM software

Zeal — installs on payment terminals to identify customers at checkout, run loyalty programmes, collect feedback, show customer profiles in real time.

☁️ 4. All-in-one POS + CRM + payments

  • SpotOn — POS, payments, CRM, loyalty, analytics
  • ConnectPOS — centralises customer data, sales, automation

🧭 Quick way to choose

  • Simplest setup? → Shopify POS, Clover
  • Already have terminals? → Add CRM via integrations
  • CRM directly on the terminal? → Zeal
  • Multi-location? → SpotOn, ConnectPOS
